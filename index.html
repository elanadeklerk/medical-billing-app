How to set up a new doctor:
1. Doctor creates a Google Form for patient intake and links it to a Sheet.
2. Make that Sheet publicly viewable (Share → Anyone with link → Viewer).
3. Copy the Sheet ID from the URL: docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/[ID here]/edit
4. In the Sheet, go to Extensions → Apps Script, paste the billing script, deploy it, copy the URL.
5. Fill in the form below and click Save.
